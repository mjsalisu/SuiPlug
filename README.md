SuiPlug is a live electronics marketplace that bridges real-world shopping with blockchain-powered ownership. Operating successfully in Nigeria, the platform allows customers to purchase smartphones, laptops, and gaming devices using SUI or USDT. Upon checkout, every transaction automatically mints a unique, permanent NFT receipt with decentralized metadata secured by Walrus.
By placing ownership records on-chain, SuiPlug solves critical retail pain points: traditional receipts are easily lost or altered, and verifying authenticity in secondary markets is notoriously difficult. SuiPlug transforms physical devices into verifiable digital identities, unlocking transparent warranty tracking, effortless product resale, and unmatched consumer trust.
Built on the Sui network, the platform leverages an object-centric architecture, lightning-fast speeds, and minimal gas fees to deliver a smooth checkout experience that rivals traditional e-commerce giants.
As part of our hackathon vision, we are scaling SuiPlug into the ultimate Web3 gateway through several key upgrades:
zkLogin Integration: Allowing frictionless user onboarding using standard social accounts—no seed phrases required.
Dynamic NFTs: Enabling receipts to automatically update as warranties age or repairs occur.
Fiat On-Ramps: Lowering barriers to entry for everyday consumers.
Advanced Analytics: Powered by Sui indexing infrastructure to provide data-driven insights.
SuiPlug is moving blockchain beyond speculation into practical, daily utility. By transforming every purchase into a secure digital asset, we are making verifiable ownership the new global standard for commerce and onboarding millions of mainstream users into the Sui ecosystem.
## Features

- **NFT Ownership**: Each product purchase is backed by an NFT to verify ownership.
- **Crypto Payments**: Make seamless payments using popular cryptocurrencies.
- **Real-Time Inventory**: Track the availability of products in real-time.
- **Decentralized Reviews**: Leave trusted reviews verified by the blockchain.
- **Wallet-Based Accounts**: Manage your account through a crypto wallet for added security.
